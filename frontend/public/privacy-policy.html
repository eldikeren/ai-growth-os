Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Elad Digital ("we", "us", "our") operates the AI Growth OS platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use AI Growth OS, we may collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, company name, website URL
  • Service credentials: API keys and OAuth tokens for connected platforms (Google, Meta, DataForSEO, Moz, OpenAI) — stored encrypted using AES-256-CBC
  • Platform data: SEO metrics, keyword rankings, PageSpeed scores, review counts, and other marketing analytics fetched from connected APIs
  • Usage data: Agent run history, approval actions, audit trail entries

3. Data from Third-Party Platforms

When you connect third-party services (such as Facebook, Instagram, Google), we access data in accordance with their respective APIs and terms of service. This includes:

  • Facebook/Meta: Page insights, follower counts, post engagement metrics. We request only the permissions necessary to provide our service.
  • Google: Search Console data, Analytics metrics, Business Profile reviews, PageSpeed scores.

We do not sell any data obtained from third-party platforms.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered SEO and marketing services
  • To run automated agent analyses and generate insights and recommendations
  • To store and display your marketing metrics and KPIs
  • To send notifications about agent runs, approvals, and system status
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and improve our platform

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security. All API credentials are encrypted at rest using AES-256-CBC encryption. Our application is hosted on Vercel with HTTPS encryption in transit.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), OpenAI (AI processing) — only as necessary to operate the platform
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account termination, all associated data will be deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to connected third-party services at any time
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
